*A send-ready one-pager for the first paid STG engagement, plus the commercial terms and the
retained-IP contract clause. Pair with `docs/commercial-strategy.md` (the why) and `docs/outreach.md`
(the intro).*

**How to use this**
- Part A is **client-facing** — adapt the bracketed bits and send/say it after the 30-min call.
- Pick **one** exposure. Recommended: the **EU tobacco-tax revision** (your cigar/pipe core, ~85% of the business) or the **France oral-nicotine ban** (dual cigar + pouch exposure, and an in-force deadline that makes it urgent).
- Price: a **single fixed fee**. Recommended anchor **DKK 110,000**; the landable band is **DKK 95–150k**. Set it **below the sponsor's confirmed discretionary signing threshold** (ask on the call) so one person approves it without procurement. Don't quote a day rate or an hour count.

- ⚠️ Part C is a **draft, not legal advice** — have a Danish advokat review the konsulentaftale + DPA before you send or sign anything. The retained-IP clause is non-negotiable for you; the rest is adjustable.

## Part A — the one-pager (client-facing)

### Regulatory Impact Sprint — what [the EU tobacco-tax revision] is worth to STG, in kroner

**The problem.** STG's P&L is exposed to a moving wall of tobacco/nicotine regulation — EU excise revision, national bans and caps — and no current vendor turns any of it into a number on *STG's own* footprint. Market panels look backward; regulatory monitors stop at summaries; a bespoke economics study takes months and six figures. So the exposure gets assessed in-house, late, and without a defensible range — the kind of surprise that moves guidance.

**The sprint.** In **~3 weeks**, for a **fixed fee**, I take **one regulation** — *[the EU tobacco-tax revision (COM(2025) 580)]* — and work it all the way down to a **DKK EBITDA band on STG's footprint**: a low/base/high range, not a point estimate, with every input editable and every figure citing a public source (or marked "not published — you set it"). You've already seen the method working on public data in the prototype; this calibrates it to STG.

**What you get**
- A **finance-grade impact band** for the chosen regulation — DKK EBITDA/volume/margin at risk per year, as a defensible range.
- The **full model, in the open**: the contribution-margin walk, the editable assumptions (exposed share, pass-through, elasticity, recapture), and the source behind every number — so your team can *argue with it*, not just receive it.
- A **hosted, private link** to the live model + a **one-page board-ready export** (PDF).
- A **90-minute working session** where your strategy/finance team stress-tests the assumptions live.

**What I need from you**
- 60 minutes with someone who knows the relevant volume/margin lines.
- *(Real-data mode)* the relevant revenue/volume figures under an NDA + a short data-processing agreement — EU-hosted, bands computed and returned, raw figures never leave the processing environment.
- *(Public-data mode)* nothing — I run it on published segment data and label every derived figure as an analyst estimate.

**Price & terms.** **DKK [110,000], fixed.** One regulation, ~3 weeks, the deliverables above. Public data only, framed strictly as internal decision-support — never investor-facing. Built and delivered by Valent (valent.dk); the tool/method is licensed to STG, your data and the resulting analysis are yours (full terms overleaf).

**Why now.** In a year of ~DKK 275m special costs and a tightened payout, this is cheap insurance against the one surprise that isn't yet sized — and the natural first step before deciding whether to put your real numbers behind it.

**Next step.** Say go, pick the regulation, and I'll send a 2-page agreement and start. → [nazar@valent.dk] · [+45 50 36 71 11]
